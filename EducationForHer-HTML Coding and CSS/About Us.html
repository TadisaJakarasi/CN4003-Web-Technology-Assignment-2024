WHO WE ARE

Education For Her is dedicated to empowering girls and young women across Africa through comprehensive education and mentorship programs. Our primary focus is on securing financial assistance from donors to provide access to quality education for underserved communities. By partnering with donors and supporters, we bridge the gap in STEM education, offering scholarships and mentorship opportunities to talented young women. Beyond academic support, we nurture holistic development, instilling confidence and resilience in our students. With transparency and integrity as our guiding principles, we work tirelessly to maximize our impact and create a brighter future for girls and young women in Africa.

OUR MISSION

To empower girls and young women through holistic education and mentorship, fostering resilience, confidence, and meaningful connections for lifelong success.

OUR VISION

A world where every girl and young woman has access to quality education and mentorship, equipping them to thrive and make a positive impact.

OUR CORE VALUES

Holistic Development: Nurturing the whole individual for personal growth and resilience.
Empowerment: Providing tools, resources, and support networks for success.
Inclusivity: Advocating for equal access to education and mentorship.
Integrity: Upholding transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct.
Collaboration: Maximizing impact through partnerships and collaboration.
