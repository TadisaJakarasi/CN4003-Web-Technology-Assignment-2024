MENTORSHIP AND VOLUNTEER OPPORTUNITIES

Volunteer

    Why become a volunteer?

  • To directly empower girls and young women in Africa by supporting their access to education and opportunities for a brighter future.

  • Experience personal growth and fulfillment as you contribute your skills and passion to a meaningful cause, making a tangible difference in the lives of others.

  • Join a dedicated community of changemakers united in the mission of empowering girls and young women. Collaborate with like-minded individuals to create lasting change and impact across Africa.

Volunteer in Africa

By joining us on the ground in Africa, as a volunteer, you'll have the chance to directly impact girls' lives through various initiatives. Get involved in organizing and facilitating education programs, community events, and advocating for financial assistance. Your hands-on contribution will play a vital role in empowering girls and young women to pursue their dreams and achieve their full potential..

Volunteer from Home

As a virtual volunteer with Education For Her, you can contribute your skills and expertise remotely to support our mission. From fundraising and grant writing to social media management and graphic design, there are numerous opportunities to get involved. By volunteering from home, you'll play a crucial role in advocating for financial assistance, organizing virtual events, and spreading awareness about our cause.

VOLUNTEER WITH US

Mentor

    Why become a Mentor?

  • Mentor with Education For Her to inspire the next generation of female leaders in Africa.

  • Make a lasting difference by providing valuable guidance and opportunities to young women, shaping their futures.

  • Experience personal growth and fulfillment as you share your knowledge and gain new perspectives through mentoring.

Mentor Requirements

Relevant Experience:Possess expertise in relevant areas such as education or leadership or career development.

Empathy and Patience: Show understanding and patience in supporting mentees' needs.

Commitment to Growth: Dedicate time and effort to mentorship, fostering mentees' growth and development.

    What you gain as a Mentor?

  • Join Education For Her as a mentor and ignite your leadership abilities while empowering the next generation of female leaders in Africa.

  • Be a catalyst for positive transformation by guiding and empowering young women, shaping their destinies for a brighter future.

  • Be part of a supportive mentorship community at Education For Her, where you'll forge valuable connections, gain new perspectives, and make a lasting impact together.
