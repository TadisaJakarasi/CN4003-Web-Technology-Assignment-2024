About Us

Education For Her empowers girls and young women in Africa through education and mentorship. With strategic fundraising, we bridge gaps in STEM education, offering scholarships and mentorship opportunities. Our holistic approach ensures positive outcomes, supported by transparent communication and dedicated volunteers. We're committed to creating a brighter future for girls across Africa

Get Involved

Mentorship: Join us in guiding and inspiring the next generation of female leaders by becoming a mentor. Share your expertise and experience to empower girls and young women through our mentorship programs.
Volunteering:  From event support to program assistance, there are various opportunities to contribute your time and skills to our mission of empowering girls through education.
